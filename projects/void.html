Do Not Enter the Void

PERSONAL PROJECT 2020

In a world where collectivism prevails, individuality is suppressed, and voices differing from the ruling party are silenced, what truly affects us? Is it the "illusory grandeur" of the collective, or the human brilliance embodied in each specific, individual life around us?

This project centers on Existentialism, drawing inspiration from Absurdist Theater and DADA in its artistic approach. It aims to further explore the old question: "If everything is ultimately meaningless and destined to fade away, what is still worth loving and protecting?"

Absurd Narratives Behind the Frames

Animation to Game

In 2020 I first created this work as a 3D animation. In 2024 I transformed it into a narrative game to enhance emotional interaction and immerse the audience more fully. Players must respond to the storyline to progress the narrative.

Game UI

Technical Implementation
